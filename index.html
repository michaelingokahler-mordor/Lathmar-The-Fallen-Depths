About the Game

Lathmar: The Fallen Depths is a dark, party-based dungeon RPG inspired by the classics of the 90s. Build your party, choose your path through powerful guilds, descend into dangerous depths, and survive brutal encounters in a hostile underworld.

The game keeps the old-school core intact while modernizing the surrounding systems: clearer character management, deeper progression structures, improved usability, and mechanics shaped by tabletop-style RPG logic.

Development is currently in alpha. The current focus is the core gameplay loop: dungeon exploration, tactical auto-battles, loot and item systems, character growth, and robust tools for larger handcrafted and generated dungeon scenarios.

  • Classic party-based dungeon crawling
  • Guilds, races, stats, equipment, and long-term progression
  • Dark fantasy atmosphere with retro RPG DNA
  • A tribute to the games that defined the genre
